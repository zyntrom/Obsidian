## üßë‚Äçü§ù‚Äçüßë Fundamental Duties (Article 51A)

## ‚öôÔ∏è Directive Principles of State Policy (DPSP)

**Purpose:** Guidelines for the government to promote social and economic welfare.

- Found in **Part IV (Articles 36‚Äì51)**
- **Not enforceable by courts**, but fundamental to governance.

üß† **Inspired by:** Irish Constitution

### Difference between Fundamental Rights & DPSP

|Aspect|Fundamental Rights|DPSP|
|---|---|---|

|Implementation|Immediate|Gradual|
|Legal status|Binding|Advisory|
